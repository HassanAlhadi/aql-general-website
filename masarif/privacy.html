Masarif Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 3, 2026

Overview

Masarif is a fully local expense-tracking app. We collect no personal data, operate no servers, and transmit nothing off your device. Your financial information stays on your iPhone — always.

Data We Collect

Masarif does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data entered into the app — including transaction amounts, merchant names, and budget settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

No Network Requests

The app makes no network requests of any kind. There are no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no ad networks, and no third-party libraries that phone home. You can verify this by inspecting the app in a proxy tool — you will see zero outbound connections.

iOS Shortcuts Integration

Masarif integrates with the iOS Shortcuts app to allow automatic logging of bank SMS messages. This integration works entirely on-device:

  • SMS text is passed locally from the iOS Shortcuts automation to Masarif.
  • No SMS content is sent to any server.
  • Processing happens entirely within the app's process on your device.

Data Backup

Masarif offers an optional local backup feature that exports your data as a JSON file. This file is saved to a location you choose (e.g., Files app, iCloud Drive). We have no access to this file. iCloud Drive backup, if used, is governed by Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy.

Widgets

Masarif includes a home-screen widget that displays spending totals. Widget data is shared between the app and the widget extension via an on-device App Group container — it never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Masarif is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children. Because we collect no data at all, there is nothing to delete or correct.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy in a material way, we will update the effective date above and — where applicable — notify users through the App Store release notes.
